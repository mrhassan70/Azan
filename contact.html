FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free delivery?

Yes! We offer free home delivery on all orders within Lahore city limits. Delivery charges may apply for outstation orders. Please contact us for details.

Do you offer installment plans?

Absolutely! We offer 0% markup easy installment plans through multiple partner banks including HBL, Meezan, Standard Chartered, and more. Terms and conditions apply.

Are the products genuine with manufacturer warranty?

Yes, all products sold at La Imperial are 100% genuine and come with official manufacturer warranty. We are an authorized dealer for all brands we carry.

What is your return policy?

We offer a hassle-free 7-day return and exchange policy on all products purchased from La Imperial, provided the item is in its original condition and packaging.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes! Professional installation by certified technicians is included with purchase for ACs, refrigerators, and washing machines. Our team will schedule a convenient time for you.
